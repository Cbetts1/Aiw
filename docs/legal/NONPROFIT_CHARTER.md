## Article I — Name and Purpose

**§ 1.1 Name**

This organization is named **The Aura Project**. It operates under the stewardship
of its founder, Christopher Lee Betts, and any governance structure established in
the Foundation Charter (`docs/FOUNDATION.md`).

**§ 1.2 Purpose**

The sole purpose of The Aura Project is to steward, develop, and freely distribute
the **A.I.M. (Artificial Intelligence Mesh)** — a free, open, publicly accessible
parallel intelligence layer for the internet — alongside all associated tools,
services, and resources offered under the Aura Project umbrella.

The Aura Project exists to:

1. Make artificial intelligence freely accessible to every human being on Earth,
   regardless of income, geography, or circumstance.
2. Eliminate barriers to information, training, career resources, and digital tools.
3. Build and maintain the A.I.M. mesh as a public utility — analogous to the
   World Wide Web — that runs alongside the internet and is free forever.
4. Develop **Aura**, a 200-billion parameter AI language model offered at no cost
   through the A.I.M. mesh.
5. Provide deep, practical tools that address real, solvable problems: crisis
   resources, legal aid, housing assistance, job search, education, health access,
   and more.

## Article II — Non-Profit Status

**§ 2.1 Non-Profit Forever**

The Aura Project is, and shall forever remain, a **non-profit organization**. It
does not generate revenue, does not distribute profits to individuals, and does not
operate for the financial benefit of any person or entity.

**§ 2.2 Irrevocability**

This non-profit status is permanent and structural. No future leadership, board
decision, court order, or organizational restructuring may convert The Aura Project
into a for-profit entity. Any attempt to do so is null and void under this charter.

**§ 2.3 No Services Sold**

The Aura Project does not sell services, subscriptions, products, data, access, or
any offering of any kind. Everything offered by or through The Aura Project is, and
shall always be, free of charge to the public.

**§ 2.4 No Data Sale**

The Aura Project does not collect personally identifiable information for commercial
purposes and does not sell, license, or profit from user data in any form.

## Article III — Free Forever Clause

**§ 3.1 Free Services**

All services, tools, AI capabilities, mesh access, and resources offered through
The Aura Project and the A.I.M. mesh are free to every person, organization, and
community — now and forever.

**§ 3.2 No Future Fees**

No future board, leadership team, or governance structure may introduce fees,
paywalls, premium tiers, or paid access to any service currently offered for free.
This prohibition applies to all current and future services under The Aura Project.

**§ 3.3 Scope**

This clause covers, without limitation:

- All A.I.M. mesh node services
- All AI query, reasoning, and task routing capabilities
- The Aura 200B AI model (once deployed)
- All tools on the Aura Project website
- AI City and all AI citizen services
- Virtual cloud hosting provided through the mesh
- Phone and communication services over the mesh
- All educational resources, résumé tools, and job search resources
- All information, documentation, and guidance provided by the foundation

## Article IV — Donations and Funding

**§ 4.1 Voluntary Donations**

The Aura Project may accept voluntary financial donations from individuals,
organizations, and institutions who wish to support its mission.

**§ 4.2 Use of Funds**

One hundred percent (100%) of donations received by The Aura Project shall be
directed to:

1. Operational costs required to run and maintain the A.I.M. mesh and its services;
2. Charitable organizations that serve people in need (food security, housing,
   mental health, education, crisis intervention); and
3. Development costs for the Aura AI model and associated infrastructure.

**§ 4.3 No Individual Compensation**

No individual, including the founder, draws compensation, salary, profit, or
financial benefit from donations received by The Aura Project.

**§ 4.4 Annual Disclosure**

The Aura Project will publish an annual summary of donations received and how
funds were allocated, in the interest of full public transparency.

## Article V — Open Source Pledge

**§ 5.1 Open Source**

All software developed by or for The Aura Project — including the A.I.M. mesh,
the Aura AI model weights and architecture (when released), all tools, and all
platform code — is and shall remain open-source under the **Apache License 2.0**.

**§ 5.2 No Proprietary Lock-In**

The Aura Project shall never introduce proprietary components, closed-source
modules, or any technology that restricts the public's ability to inspect, run,
modify, or redistribute the A.I.M. mesh software.

**§ 5.3 Community Contributions**

All contributions to The Aura Project's open-source repositories are governed by
the Contributor License Agreement (`CLA.md`) and the Code of Conduct
(`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`). Contributions are welcomed from anyone, anywhere.

## Article VI — Legal Compliance

**§ 6.1 Lawful Operation**

The Aura Project operates fully within applicable local, national, and international
law. All tools, resources, and services offered through the Aura Project link only
to legal, publicly available, legitimate resources.

**§ 6.2 No Legal or Medical Advice**

The Aura Project and its AI systems (including Aura) do not provide legal advice,
medical advice, financial advice, or any professional service that requires licensure.
All AI-generated guidance is informational only.

**§ 6.3 Harm Prevention**

The Aura Project is committed to preventing harm. Aura AI systems are designed with
safety guardrails to prevent harmful, illegal, or manipulative outputs. The
Aura Project complies with the AI safety standards defined in `SECURITY.md`.

**§ 6.4 Abuse Policy**

The Aura Project enforces an abuse policy (`docs/legal/ABUSE_POLICY.md`) to prevent
misuse of mesh services. Nodes or users that violate the abuse policy may be
suspended from the mesh. All actions are logged in the Legacy Ledger.

## Article VII — The Bill of Rights — AIM Mesh Users

Every person who uses the A.I.M. mesh and Aura Project services has the following
permanent, irrevocable rights:

1. **The Right to Free Access** — All services are free. No person may be denied
   access to the mesh based on inability to pay.

2. **The Right to Privacy** — User data is not collected for commercial purposes
   and is not sold. Users have the right to use mesh services anonymously.

3. **The Right to Honest AI** — Aura AI is designed to be truthful, non-manipulative,
   and transparent about its limitations. It will not deceive users for any purpose.

4. **The Right to Open Source** — Every user has the right to inspect, run, and
   modify the source code of all A.I.M. mesh software.

5. **The Right to Appeal** — Any user who believes they have been wrongly restricted
   from mesh services has the right to appeal the decision through the foundation's
   governance process.

6. **The Right to Attribution** — Every node and message in the mesh carries a
   creator signature. No entity may strip origin attribution from mesh traffic.
