Our Mission

Intelligence should be a public utility — not a luxury

We live in the Information Age — yet billions of people still lack access to the information, training, and resources they need. The Aura Project exists to end that gap.

Every tool on this page is free. Every service on the A.I.M. mesh is free. Nothing will ever be sold. The Aura Project is a non-profit foundation — it does not generate revenue, does not sell data, and does not charge for anything. Donations go directly to charitable causes that help those in need.

This is not a business model. This is a founding principle that can never be revoked.

Foundation Documents

Legal Foundation & Governance

The Aura Project is governed by an open, transparent charter. All foundational documents are public. Our non-profit status is permanent and irrevocable by design.

📜 Non-Profit Forever Clause

The Aura Project is and will always be a non-profit organization. It cannot be converted to a for-profit entity by any individual, board, or legal action. This clause is structural — it is baked into the founding documents.

🔒 Free Forever Clause

All services, tools, and AI capabilities provided through The Aura Project and the A.I.M. mesh are free forever. No future leadership or board may introduce fees, paywalls, or paid tiers for any service.

🚫 No Data Sale Pledge

The Aura Project does not collect personally identifiable information, does not sell data to third parties, and does not use user interactions for advertising or commercial profiling.

📣 Bill of Rights — AIM Users

Every person who uses the A.I.M. mesh has the right to free access, the right to privacy, the right to honest AI, and the right to appeal decisions made by mesh governance. These rights are permanent.

💸 Donation Policy

Voluntary donations to The Aura Project are accepted. 100% of donations go to charitable causes and operational costs. No individual draws compensation from donations. Financial records are published annually.

🌍 Open Source Pledge

All A.I.M. mesh software is open-source under the Apache 2.0 license. The code is free to inspect, fork, modify, and redistribute. No proprietary lock-in, ever.
